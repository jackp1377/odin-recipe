Today we will be sauteeing various items including but not limited to:

  • carrits
  • bacon
  • eggs
  • salt and other condiments/spices

here's an approximate image of what you'll end up with

how 2 make:

  1. oil in pan (I should've mentioned you'd need that yeah)
  2. turn it to hot. then slice bacon and put it in
  3. slice up carrots and all your other ingredients too. put them in when ready
  4. whisk the eggs and put them in
  5. keep the eggs mobile so they dont burn but dont scramble them too soon! we want some strong, cohesive eggs
  6. put in your spices and enjoy!
