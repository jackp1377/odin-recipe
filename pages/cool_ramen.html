Ramen!

Here's a real life image of what you'll be making

Here's how to do it:

ingredients:

  • Maruchan ramen
  • salt, pepper, whatever other condiments or spices
  • bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • carrots and celery
  • small pot in which you cook
alright gang let's chef up! how 2 make it:
  1. first boil some water
  2. put the noodles in there. whilst they grow softer, cut some bacon into little bits (but not too little!)
  3. when the noodles are quite softened, drain most of the water. then put the bacon in. leave it on high so the bacon gets cooked well!!
  4. cut up carrots and celery, then put it in. by this point you may want to add a little more water
  5. crack eggs directly into your concoction! leave it on high so they cook. don't dash them - we want them to be kinda solid
  6. after some cooking, add your condiments. then put the flavor packet in. now we're done!
